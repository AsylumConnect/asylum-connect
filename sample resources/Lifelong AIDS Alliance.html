• Description: The Lifelong Dental Program (LDP) is a newly established, comprehensive oral health access program for low-income individuals living with HIV/AIDS who reside in King, Island, and Snohomish Counties.
  • Population Served: To be eligible for the program, clients must:
    • Be a resident of King, Island, or Snohomish Counties
    • Have an income less than 300% of Federal Poverty Level
    • Be HIV positive
    • More information about establishing eligibility here.
  • Mailing Address: Lifelong Dental Program, PO Box 80547 Seattle, WA 98108
  • Phone Number: Questions regarding the Lifelong Dental Program? Please call the LDP Coordinator at 206-957-1716 or send us a message online here. We are receiving dozens of calls a day and may not be able to call you back right away, but we will return your call within 48 hours.
  • Website: http://www.llaa.org/services/lifelong-dental-program
  • Additional Information: Eligible clients should contact their case manager for assistance applying to the LDP. Those who are not currently working with a case manager should contact the LDP coordinator at (206) 957-1716 to apply. The LDP coordinator will work with enrolled clients to set an appointment with a participating dental clinic. Application available online here.
